I'm a **BI & Data Analyst** based in **Malabe, Sri Lanka 🇱🇰**, specializing in turning raw data into clear, decision-ready insights. Currently working as an **Executive Data Analyst at Medfuture Healthcare**, where I build live dashboards, automate data pipelines, and support operational decision-making through real-time analytics.

- 🎓 BSc (Hons) in Information Technology — **SLIIT** (GPA 3.1/4.0)
- 💼 Experience across **Healthcare, Consulting (Deloitte)**, and **Digital sectors**
- 📊 Passionate about **Power BI, SQL, Python, and storytelling with data**
- 🔍 Strong interest in **data-driven process improvement and business intelligence**
